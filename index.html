Meet Erin

Politics

Erin started as a Politics major and added Sociology to better understand society. Her core requirements led her to minor in Peace and Justice.

Dr. Boryczka

While pursuing this minor, she met Dr. Boryczka, who showed her different ways to see the world and question it – a process that led her to adding an additional Irish Studies minor.

Fulbright Scholar

She's now determined to fix our failing prison system. As a Fulbright Scholar she's living in Ireland and studying its progressive re-entry programs in hopes of unlocking a solution.

A double major and double minor is more than ambition. It's an awakening.

Erin Eife started off with one major, in politics, and an interest in shaping society. But she didn't stop there.
